About

A weekend retreat for programmers that want to learn from one another.

Craft the codes, spend some time in nature, and check out what Floyd has to offer.

Location

Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia, Floyd is a small mountain town with class.

Environment

Ruby will be the main language of the retreat. Other languages are welcomed, especially if you have a pair using the same language to work with. This Code Retreat is not intended to be a language learning day, rather a crafting/honing of what you know.

Schedule

We'll start our journey on Saturday coding at the Sun Music Hall in downtown Floyd. We'll then move to Apple Ridge for a full catered pizza/beer social Saturday night and more pair programming on Sunday. We'll be providing delicious local, organic food and snacks throughout the weekend.

View Full Schedule

Travel

Airport: The closest airport is Roanoke, Va (ROA). If you are renting a car, Greensboro, NC (GSO) may be cheaper.

Hotels: The Hotel Floyd is closest to the The Sun Music Hall. The Bent Mountain Lodge is the closest to Apple Ridge. Hotels in Roanoke are the closest to the airport.
