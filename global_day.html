Welcome to the Global Day of Coderetreat! December 3rd, 2011

What is it?

Global Day of Coderetreat is a world-wide event celebrating passion and software craftsmanship. Over 2000 passionate software developers around the world will spend the day practicing the craft of software development using the coderetreat format. You can find out more information about the day on the coderetreat blog.

Please see our press release

Where is it?

Global Day of Coderetreat currently has over 80 cities registered around the globe, and we are organizing more cities all the time. You can find out specific details of whether there is an event in your city on the current global day event listing.


View Global Day of Coderetreat in a larger map

Where will Corey be?

In order to raise awareness, Corey Haines will be facilitating the first coderetreat in Sydney, Australia, then hopping a 6pm flight that lands in Honolulu, Hawaii, at 6.45am on the same day. He'll then facilitate the last coderetreat of the day.

You could be a sponsor
